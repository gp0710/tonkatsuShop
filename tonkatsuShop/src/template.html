Ah-mei's Crispy Fried Pork Cutlet est. 1983

Since 1983, Ah-Mei has been frying delicious taiwanese-style fried pork cutlets out of her little food cart on YongKang street.

Locals and tourists alike would be attracted to the fragrant aroma of the peanut oil and sizzling sound of her signature crispy & juicy pork cutlet.

What started out as a small street cart has now expanded to over 10 stores in Taiwan.
